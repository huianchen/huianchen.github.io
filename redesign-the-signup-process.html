Redesign the signup process

Launched in May 2020

The original registration process only allowed users to use their ID number to sign up. Besides, the users had to provide their email addresses. However, it discouraged a lot of people from becoming a member. Hence, the Tasker team decided to redesign the signup process and make the users more willing to register Tasker.

Solution

Provide users with two simple ways to sign up for Tasker (register by a phone number or log in with a Facebook/Google account) and allowing the users to integrate their original account with Facebook/Google account so that they can log in faster.

Then, I designed a user flow to discuss with developers:

Mockup

First, enter a phone number, and then key in the verification code sent by SMS. Second, set up a password and finish the registration.

When signing up with a Facebook account, users can connect with their original account by entering their ID number/phone number and password.
