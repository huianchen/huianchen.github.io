About

“Product design is what I take pride in.” When talking about product design, there is always a spark in my eyes.

For me, discovering users’ needs to finally satisfy their requirements is a fascinating process. Not only does it give me a sense of achievement, but it also arouses my passion for learning. That is, when brainstorming solutions with partners, it’s interesting to learn many different perspectives. Also, to make users’ experiences better, I am always eager to gain knowledge about design trends and best practices.

I consider myself a curious person, and there is no doubt that product design can satisfy my curiosity. Similarly, when I’m not working, I like to try new things such as running a half-marathon, biking up a mountain, or attending a dance class.
