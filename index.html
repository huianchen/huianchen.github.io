Projects

Redesign the signup process

The original registration process only allowed users to use their ID number to sign up. Besides, the users had to provide their email addresses. However, it has discouraged a lot of people from becoming a member. Hence, the Tasker team decided to redesign the signup process and make the users more willing to register Tasker. Launched in May 2020

Create an instant chat system

Looking for a freelancer on a platform is like searching for products on an e-commerce site. First, they browse freelancers’ artwork (products). Second, when they see the one they like, they use an instant chat system to ask freelancers (sellers) for more information. Thus, the instant chat system seems to play an important role in this process. Launched in Jan 2020

Redesign the portfolio page

By organizing the feedback given by freelancers, I discovered several problems that freelancers have encountered while editing their portfolios on Tasker Launched in Sep 2019
